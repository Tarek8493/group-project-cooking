Safety

Cooking is a essential skill to learn. However, working in close proximity to heat can be dangerous.

Here are some statistics of the most common cooking injuries:

Injury Likelihood
Burns
  • 486,000 burn injuries occur per year!
  • Human exposure to hot water at 140Â°F can lead to a serious burn within three seconds.
  • 72% of burns occur at home!
Cuts
  • 350,000 people are injured by kitchen knives each year.
  • Cuts are among the most common cooking injuries.
  • Dull knives are more dangerous than sharp ones!
Falls
  • 67 % of workers in wet kitchens reported slips and falls at least once per month.
  • 92 % of those working in wet kitchens said that the floor was slippery during the week.
  • Hazards include greasy floors, low lighting, and loose mats.
