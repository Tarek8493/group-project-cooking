Safety

Cooking is a essential skill to learn. However, working in close proximity to heat can be dangerous.

Thus, we created a guide to preventing the most common kitchen injuries:

Injury Prevention Methods
Burns
  • Never leave stove unattended
  • Cook while awake and alert
  • Move flammable materials
Cuts
  • Use cutting boards
  • Cook while awake and alert
