Cooking Tips

This page provides various cooking tips to help you when preparing recipes!

Specifically, this page covers important safety precautions. Hover over each image for tips!

Tips

Never leave a fire unattended.

Wear oven mits when touching something hot.

Keep flammable objects away from the stove.


Use a cutting board when cutting food.

Cut away from, not towards, your body.

Curl your fingers while chopping.


Clean up spills immediately.

Avoid running in kitchens.

Watch where you step!
